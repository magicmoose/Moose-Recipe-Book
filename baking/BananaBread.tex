\recipe{Banana Bread}
\ingred{
\begin{tabular}{l l}
1 \sfrac{3}{4} cups & flour\\
2 tsp& baking powder\\
\sfrac{1}{4} tsp& baking soda\\
\sfrac{1}{2} tsp& salt\\
2 & eggs\\
\sfrac{2}{3} cup& granulated sugar\\
\sfrac{1}{3} cup& olive or veg oil\\
1 cup& mashed banana (2-3)\\
\sfrac{2}{3} cup& chocolate chips (optional)
\end{tabular}
}
\img{20101228_TestCreate_043.jpg}
Prehead oven to 350\degree F

Butter and lighly flour the baking dish.

Mix together flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt

In large bowl with electric mixer beat eggs with sugar until doubled in volume. 

Alternately stir in dry ingredients and banana\&oil puree. Optional add chocolate chips. 

Pour batter into mold and bake for 55 min
